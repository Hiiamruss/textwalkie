How to use Walkie-Talkie?

To use the walkie talkie, enter a room id. (This could be any word, nothing is wrong.)

If you want to communicate with other person, make sure they join the same room as you.

Then, enter your name. This will show in the reciever's listner, like, "[yourname] said: [message]"

Then type in your message at the box below. This will send the message to everyone in the room you are in.
