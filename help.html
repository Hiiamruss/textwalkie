How to use Walkie-Talkie?

Choose a channel from the dropdown. We supply 5 public servers with one developer server.

If you want to communicate with other person, make sure they join the same channel as you.

Then, enter your name. This will show in the reciever's listner, like, "[yourname] said: [message]"

Then type in your message at the box below. This will send the message to everyone in the channel you are in. Just like a real walkie talkie!
